ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Started in 2019, XVERSION is a music band with the motto of "Rediscovering Music". Our sole purpose is to make you FEEL each and every beat of a song. We mainly express ourselves through VIOLIN and GUITAR.
Apart from popular covers, we also make our own music - the proper writing-composition-production of music. We currently have 3 original tracks. With all this, we have never stopped and will never stop making and expressing good music!

THE TEAM

AYUSH BORA Guitarist

Ayush is very passionate person towards music.He is a certified "Grade 5 Guitarist" from Trinity College of London. He is more interested in building tracks and mashups. He also has a great love for guitar. He is also a great foodie. His dream is to make XVERSION the bigest band in the world. Ayush feels the music in each and every part of his soul. He is a fast learner. He always try to learn new instruments. He has produced music for three originals of XVERSION. His favourite genre is HIP-HOP. He is an amazing guy to be with.

ABHISHEK BORA Violinist

On of the most charming personalities you'll ever come across! Abhishek believes in living the life to the fullest. His love and passion for music is reflected in all of his performances. Abhishek is also, as he says - "World's best bathroom singer". His dream is to make XVERSION the world's most loved band. He is also fond of writing and composing songs and till now he has 3 originals under the name of XVERSION. His favourite genre is POP MUSIC and he always strives for perfection.
